sound: pitch shift

Pitch shift is a sound variation in which the pitch of a sound (quantified as frequency, unit: Hz) is raised or lowered.

interaction: on\off

on-off interaction refers to a controller, allowing turning a device or a function on or off.

examples
can be attached as a feedback for the "on-off" button in electric devices such as a remote, or a kettle.

NOTICE! make sure the chosen pitches lay within the average audible frequency range for your user age group.
